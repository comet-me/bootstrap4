Pass

A Pass occurs when the quarterback throws or pitches the ball forward.

A Pass results in one of the following:

  • Complete - Caught by the offensive receiver
  • Incomplete - Dropped/not caught by the offensive receiver
  • Intercepted - Caught by the defensive team in the air (or steal in basketball)

A pass is always thrown forward. If a ball is thrown directly to the side or backwards, it will not be a pass, but a run instead.

Complete

Complete is used on passing plays only; when the ball is thrown forward from the original ball handler. A completion occurs when a pass is caught inbounds by a member of the offensive team.
Possible Corresponding Play Types: Pass, Fake Punt and Fake FG Result: Complete
